LOGO CONVERSION INSTRUCTIONS:

To enable logo printing on thermal receipts:

1. Convert the SVG logo to PNG:
   - Use any online SVG to PNG converter
   - Set width to 400px (optimal for 80mm thermal paper)
   - Ensure it's black and white (no grayscale)
   - Save as: assets/logo.png

2. Enable PNG in pubspec.yaml:
   - Uncomment the line: # - assets/logo.png

3. The invoice service will automatically detect and use the PNG logo
